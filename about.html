About us

Gospel Crusaders Campus Fellowship is an interdenominational fellowship registerd with the University of Ilorin, that brings people with similar interest (majorly students - both Undergraduates and Post Graduates) together solely for the purpose of having fellowship with one another and rendering worship to God. The Fellowship started in 2008/2009 session with three persons and has over the years produced graduates who are balanced, i.e zealous for God, successful academically, equipped with all that is needed for life, impacting the world anywhere they are and having a glorious future ahead of them. We perform functions such as: Studying of God's Word, Prayers, Round-Table Discussions, Organizing Conferences, Counselling and Consultations, coupled with Tutorials and Monitoring of results by the Academic Director for the benefits of the students.

Aims and Objectives

Gospel Crusaders Campus Fellowship aims to:

  • Extend the gospel of our Lord and saviour Jesus Christ to higher institutions without neglecting the sound truth of the scripture
  • Enhance Students' spiritual life according to God's standard despite the degradation of moral standard and multiplication of immoralities in higher institutions.
  • Equipp Students in attaining zenith in their academic pursuit in Life. Excellence
  • Preparing them for future challenges

Becoming a member of GCCF will not only add value to your life spiritually and academically, but it will also link you to other students with great and glorious destinies.

We are people of the WORD, impacting the WORLD, and we are concerned about the academic, spiritual, as well as physical welfare of all memebers.
